# Citation Standard

A technical claim cites source ID, URL, source version, retrieval time, content hash when available, applicability, confidence and freshness.

A citation proves provenance, not truth. The claim still requires contextual validation.

Schema: `.mcp/schemas/citation.schema.json`. Builder: `sources.cite` in [[Source MCP]].
