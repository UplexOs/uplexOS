# Data Classification

- `public`: approved for public distribution.
- `internal`: normal organizational knowledge.
- `confidential`: business or customer-sensitive data requiring minimization and controlled access.
- `restricted`: secrets or highly sensitive information; external transfer denied by default.

Secrets never belong in the vault. Classification does not replace legal analysis or consent.
