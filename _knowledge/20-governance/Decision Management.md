# Decision Management

A decision note records context, alternatives, decision, consequences, owner and status. Changed decisions are superseded, not silently rewritten.

Use ADRs for durable architecture choices. Use project decisions for scoped product or operational choices. Hypotheses remain proposed until confirmed.
