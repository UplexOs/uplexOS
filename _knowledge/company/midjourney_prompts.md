# 3 Prompts for Gemini (Midjourney-style, Photorealistic)

**Prompt 1: O Motor UplexOS (The Autonomic Engine)**
A hyper-realistic editorial photography shot inside a modern, high-tech server room mixed with a sleek hacker workspace. A matte black metal desk with a single ultra-wide curved monitor displaying streams of green and white code on a dark terminal. The environment is dimly lit with subtle neon blue and soft amber LED strips reflecting off frosted glass panels. No humans present, giving an autonomic, self-building system vibe. A subtle, elegant physical metallic logo plaque (space reserved for UplexOS logo) sits on the corner of the desk. Shot on 35mm lens, Sony A7R IV, natural lighting with subtle shadows, raw photo, corporate realism, unedited, no 3D render, no CGI, strictly photorealistic.

**Prompt 2: O Sucesso do Aluno (The First Client Milestone)**
A highly detailed, photorealistic close-up of a premium, modern wooden office desk. Resting on the desk is a sleek, brushed aluminum award plaque that reads "Primeiro Cliente" in elegant typography. Next to the plaque is a matte black coffee mug with steam rising slightly, and a closed premium laptop. The lighting is natural morning sunlight pouring in through a large window out of frame, casting long, soft, realistic shadows across the desk's wood grain. Space reserved for a logo in the top center of the composition. Shot on 35mm lens, Sony A7R IV, natural lighting, raw photo, corporate realism, unedited, no 3D render, no CGI, strictly photorealistic.

**Prompt 3: A Arquitetura Enterprise (The Physical Architecture)**
An ultra-realistic, low-angle editorial photograph of an enterprise datacenter aisle. Rows of massive, matte black server racks with brushed steel accents. Subtle, real-world imperfections like slight dust motes in the air and realistic cable management textures. The lighting is dramatic but realistic, featuring cool white server indicator lights and soft, diffused overhead industrial lighting creating deep, natural shadows. A reserved blank illuminated frosted glass panel on the side of the closest rack for logo placement. Shot on 35mm lens, Sony A7R IV, natural lighting, raw photo, corporate realism, unedited, no 3D render, no CGI, strictly photorealistic.
