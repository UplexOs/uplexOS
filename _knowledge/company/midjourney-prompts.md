# Prompts Prontos para Geração de Imagens

Como o Claude Code não gera pixels graficamente sem uma API externa configurada (como DALL-E), utilize as ferramentas gratuitas ou o Midjourney com os prompts abaixo.

## Capa de Curso - O Diretor de Tecnologia (Autoridade Executiva)

**Copie e cole este comando no Midjourney, Bing Image Creator ou Leonardo AI:**

> A hyper-realistic editorial portrait for a premium masterclass course cover. A confident, modern tech entrepreneur (male, mid-30s, sharp features, wearing a sleek high-quality dark grey t-shirt and an elegant minimalist wristwatch) standing in a high-end dark tech office. He is looking slightly off-camera with a focused, intelligent expression. The background features dark frosted glass panels and blurred, glowing server lights in deep blue and purple tones, completely out of focus (beautiful bokeh). The subject is positioned on the right side of the frame (rule of thirds), leaving massive dark negative space on the left side for large typography. Cinematic rim lighting on his shoulder and hair. Shot on 50mm lens, Sony A7R IV, f/1.4, raw photography, unedited, hyper-detailed skin texture, strictly photorealistic, no CGI, corporate leadership aesthetic --ar 16:9

## Capa de Curso - Mão na Massa (Imersão)

> A photorealistic cinematic wide shot for a premium tech academy cover art. A modern tech founder sitting at a sleek matte-black metal desk in a dimly lit, high-end office, intensely focused on typing on a brushed aluminum laptop. The glow from an unseen monitor softly illuminates his face. Behind him is a completely dark room with very subtle, elegant neon cyan accent lights reflecting off dark wood panels. The composition places the person on the left side, leaving deep, solid dark negative space on the right and top for bold text placement. Shot on 35mm lens, Sony A7R IV, f/1.8, dramatic lighting, sharp focus on the person, realistic skin texture and fabric, raw photography, no plastic AI look, strictly photorealistic --ar 16:9
