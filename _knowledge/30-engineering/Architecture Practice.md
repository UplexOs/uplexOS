# Architecture Practice

Inspect the repository and constraints before proposing architecture. Document context, quality attributes, alternatives, trade-offs, boundaries, data ownership, integration failure modes and deployment implications.

Do not impose a universal stack. Record durable choices through [[Decision Management]]. Route implementation through [[Tech Lead]] and operational constraints through [[Operations Map]].
