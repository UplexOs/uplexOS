# Technology Radar

The radar records project-independent posture without declaring a universal stack.

## Rings

- [[Radar - Adopt]] — supported defaults when requirements match.
- [[Radar - Trial]] — controlled use with explicit evaluation.
- [[Radar - Assess]] — research before production use.
- [[Radar - Hold]] — avoid for new work unless constrained.
- [[Version Watch]] — detected versions, support and migration signals.

Movement between rings requires evidence and a decision through [[Decision Management]]. Sources come from [[Sources Map]].
