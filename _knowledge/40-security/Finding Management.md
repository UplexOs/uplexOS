# Finding Management

A finding records stable ID, category, severity, confidence, location, redacted evidence, preconditions, plausible attack path, impact, remediation, verification, owner, status and residual risk.

Potential findings are validated before release decisions. False positives retain rationale. Accepted findings link to [[Risk Management]] and [[Approval Model]].
